Lasagna

Easy lasagna but taste delicious

Just like momma us to make and great for dinners or potlucks

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mozzerella
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Lean ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350f
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. Mix the two cheeses together
  4. Spread meat, sauce, and cheese in baking dish
  5. Create layers with noodles and mixture
  6. Bake for 45 minutes uncovered
